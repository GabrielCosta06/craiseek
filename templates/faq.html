Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Marketseek rental alerts.

Getting Started

What is Marketseek? +

Marketseek is a real-time rental listing alert service that monitors Facebook Marketplace for new apartment and rental postings. We scan Facebook Marketplace every few minutes and send instant alerts via SMS, WhatsApp, or email so you can respond to listings before everyone else.

How do I sign up? +

Simply visit our Plans page, choose your preferred tier (Free, Essential, or Elite), and provide your contact information. For paid plans, you'll complete checkout through Stripe. For the free plan, verify your email to start receiving daily digests.

Which plan should I choose? +

It depends on how urgently you need housing:

  • Scout (Free): Best for casual browsers who want daily email summaries
  • Essential ($3.90/month): Perfect for active searchers who need instant alerts via one channel
  • Elite ($6.90/month): Ideal for competitive markets where speed matters—get simultaneous alerts via SMS, WhatsApp, and email

How It Works

How often do you scan Facebook Marketplace? +

We scan Facebook Marketplace approximately every 5 minutes for our paid subscribers. Free tier users receive a daily digest of the top listings from the previous 24 hours.

How fast will I receive alerts? +

Essential and Elite subscribers receive alerts within seconds to minutes of a new listing appearing on Facebook Marketplace. The exact timing depends on when our scraper runs its next cycle and network delivery times.

Can I customize which neighborhoods I monitor? +

Currently, alerts include all available listings. You can filter listings on your dashboard by neighborhood, price range, and keywords. Custom neighborhood alerts are planned for a future release.

What information do alerts include? +

Each alert includes the listing title, price, neighborhood (when available), and a direct link to the Facebook Marketplace posting so you can view photos and contact the landlord immediately.

Account & Billing

How do I change my alert channel? +

For Essential subscribers, visit the Plans page to change between SMS, WhatsApp, or email. Elite subscribers receive alerts on all channels simultaneously. Changes take effect immediately.

Can I cancel anytime? +

Yes! You can cancel your subscription anytime from your Account Settings. Cancellation takes effect at the end of your current billing period. No refunds are provided for partial months.

How do I update my phone number or email? +

Contact our support team at support@Marketseek.com with your current and new contact information. We'll update your account within 24 hours.

What payment methods do you accept? +

We accept all major credit and debit cards through Stripe, including Visa, Mastercard, American Express, and Discover. All payments are processed securely and we never store your card details.

Troubleshooting

I'm not receiving alerts. What should I do? +

First, check that:

  • Your subscription is active (check Settings)
  • For email: Check your spam/junk folder and whitelist noreply@Marketseek.com
  • For SMS: Verify your phone number is correct in your account
  • There are new listings in your area (check the Dashboard)

If issues persist, contact support@Marketseek.com.

Why do some listings not have a price or neighborhood? +

We display exactly what the landlord posted on Facebook Marketplace. If they didn't include a price or neighborhood, we can't add that information. However, the listing link will take you directly to Facebook Marketplace where you can view all available details.

I received a duplicate alert. Is this a bug? +

We work hard to prevent duplicates, but occasionally landlords repost the same listing with slight changes, which our system treats as new. If you experience frequent duplicates, please let us know at support@Marketseek.com.

Safety & Privacy

How do I avoid rental scams? +

We deliver listings from Facebook Marketplace but cannot verify their legitimacy. Protect yourself by:

  • Never send money before seeing the property in person
  • Be wary of prices significantly below market rate
  • Meet landlords at the property, not remote locations
  • Don't share personal financial information upfront
  • Use Facebook Marketplace's scam warning resources
What do you do with my data? +

We only use your contact information to send rental alerts and account notifications. We never sell your data to third parties. Read our full Privacy Policy for details on data collection, storage, and your rights.

Is my payment information secure? +

Yes. All payments are processed through Stripe, a PCI-compliant payment processor used by millions of businesses. We never see or store your full card details.
