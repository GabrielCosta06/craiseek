Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 17, 2025

At Marketseek, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our rental alert service.

1. Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Account Information: Email address and password when you create an account
  • Contact Information: Phone number (for SMS/WhatsApp alerts), email address
  • Payment Information: Payment details processed securely through Stripe (we do not store credit card information)
  • Preferences: Alert channel preferences, neighborhood selections, and search criteria

Information Collected Automatically

  • Usage Data: Pages visited, features used, time spent on the platform
  • Device Information: Browser type, operating system, IP address
  • Cookies: Session cookies for authentication and user preferences (e.g., dark mode)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and deliver rental listing alerts via your chosen channels (SMS, WhatsApp, email)
  • Process your subscription payments and maintain your account
  • Personalize your experience based on your preferences
  • Send you service-related communications (billing, account updates, security alerts)
  • Improve our service through analytics and user feedback
  • Detect and prevent fraud, abuse, or security issues
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information with:

Service Providers

  • Stripe: Payment processing (subject to Stripe's Privacy Policy)
  • Twilio: SMS and WhatsApp message delivery (when configured)
  • Email Service: For sending email alerts and notifications

Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law, court order, or to protect our rights, property, or safety, or that of our users or the public.

4. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting us. Some information may be retained for legal or business purposes after account deletion.

5. Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal information we hold about you
  • Correction: Update or correct inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and personal data
  • Opt-Out: Unsubscribe from marketing emails or cancel your subscription
  • Data Portability: Request your data in a portable format

To exercise these rights, please contact us at privacy@Marketseek.com.

6. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information, including:

  • Encryption of data in transit (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Secure password hashing (bcrypt)
  • Session-based authentication with httpOnly cookies
  • Regular security updates and monitoring

However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies for:

  • Authentication: To keep you logged in across sessions
  • Preferences: To remember your theme choice (dark/light mode)
  • Analytics: To understand how users interact with our service

You can control cookies through your browser settings, but disabling cookies may affect functionality.

8. Third-Party Links

Our service includes links to Facebook Marketplace listings and other third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these external sites. Please review their privacy policies before providing any information.

9. Children's Privacy

Marketseek is not intended for users under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect information from children. If you believe we have collected information from a child, please contact us immediately.

10. California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA):

  • Right to know what personal information is collected, used, shared, or sold
  • Right to delete personal information
  • Right to opt-out of sale of personal information (we do not sell your data)
  • Right to non-discrimination for exercising your rights

11. European Privacy Rights (GDPR)

If you are in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), including the right to access, rectify, erase, restrict processing, object to processing, and data portability.

12. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date. Your continued use of Marketseek after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

13. Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us:

Email: privacy@Marketseek.com
Support: support@Marketseek.com
